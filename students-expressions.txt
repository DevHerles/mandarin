0. 课堂学生有用的表达 / Useful expressions for students in the classroom
1. 老师，你可以再说一遍吗？ / Teacher, can you say it again?
2. 请问，设个我不明白。 / Please, I don't understand.
3. 老师， 。。。用韩语怎么说？ / Teacher, 。。。 how do you say it in Korean?
4. 老师，“plátano”用中文怎么说？ / Teacher, "plátano" how do you say it in Chinese?
5. 请问，设是什么意思？ / Please, what does 设 mean?
6. 老师，我有一个问题。 / Teacher, I have a question.
7. 老师，我可以进来吗？ / Teacher, can I come in?
8. 等一下 / Wait a moment
